# Invexa – Smart Inventory & Billing Management System

## Overview

Invexa is a modern inventory and billing management system built to simplify business operations for shops, wholesalers, and small enterprises. The platform helps users manage products, categories, suppliers, invoices, reports, and stock updates through a clean and user-friendly interface.

The project focuses on improving inventory tracking, reducing manual work, and generating business insights through organized dashboards and reports.

---

# Features

## Authentication & User Management

* Secure user login and registration system
* Role-based access management
* Session handling and authorization
* User profile management

## Dashboard

* Interactive admin dashboard
* Overview of products, suppliers, invoices, and stock
* Quick business statistics and summaries
* Easy navigation across modules

## Product Management

* Add, edit, update, and delete products
* Product categorization support
* Product stock tracking
* Product search and filtering

## Category Management

* Create and manage product categories
* Organize products efficiently
* Category-wise inventory handling

## Supplier Management

* Maintain supplier records
* Store supplier contact details
* Manage supplier-product relationships

## Invoice & Billing System

* Create professional invoices
* Generate billing records
* Track invoice history
* Manage customer purchase details

## Reports & Analytics

* Inventory reports
* Sales and invoice reports
* Business insights through analytics
* Organized data visualization

## Responsive UI

* Clean and modern interface
* Mobile-friendly design
* Smooth navigation experience
