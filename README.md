# Invexa – Smart Inventory & Billing Management System

## Overview

Invexa is an inventory and billing management system designed for shops and small businesses. It helps manage products, suppliers, invoices, categories, and stock through a clean and user-friendly interface.

The project focuses on simplifying inventory tracking, billing, and business management operations.

---

## Features

### Authentication & User Management
- Secure login and registration
- Role-based access
- Session management

### Dashboard
- Business overview and statistics
- Product, supplier, and invoice summaries

### Product Management
- Add, edit, update, and delete products
- Product categorization
- Stock tracking and search

### Category Management
- Manage product categories
- Organize inventory efficiently

### Supplier Management
- Store supplier information
- Manage supplier records

### Invoice & Billing
- Generate invoices
- Track billing history
- Manage customer purchase records

### Reports & Analytics
- Inventory and sales reports
- Business analytics dashboard

### Responsive UI
- Mobile-friendly interface
- Clean and modern design
